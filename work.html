PRINTS

 

With the protection of Cape Cod's seals, comes the explosion of the population. And with seals, come the sharks. Massachusetts has had a long, tense, relationship with the animal, one which has brought forth the American classic, Jaws, and countless others which serve to depict the vilified shark as an enemy of the human race. Yet creative fanatics stir continuous fear of the animal and forever questioning of its motives off the screen. The Sharp Truth is a project which serves to combat this perspective, and highlight the truth of the shark. It includes a multitude of sources spanning from 1895 to 2020, from first person narratives to scientific facts, which all come together to cut through the noise and sketch out our conflicting reality.

DATA STRUCTURES

The Visability Project

To visually show how information filters and reaches an average peer of mine, a member of the American Gen Z, I created the Visibility Project. Using vis.js, I designed and built a node network, populating it with self-made graphics meant to depict recognizable interfaces, color-coding them to align with the intensities of visable light to the human eye.
